Homemade Potato Salad

Description

Potato salad is a salad dish made from boiled potatoes, usually containing a dressing and a variety of other ingredients such as boiled eggs and raw vegetables. In the United States, it is generally considered a side dish and usually accompanies the main course.

This recipe is designed for 6 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 1tbsp salt
  • 1tsp caraway seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 5 eggs
  • 4 onions
  • 4 pickles
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 30g sugar
  • 3tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 600ml vegetable stock
  • 1tsp mustard
  • 20g chives
  • salt
  • pepper
  • water
  • ice cubes
  1. Add potatoes to a large pot over medium high heat, and add enough water to cover. Add salt, caraway seeds, and bay leaf to pot, bring to a boil. When potatoes are boiling, reduce to a simmer and cover.
  2. While the potatoes are cooking, add eggs into a pot of simmering water and cook for appox. 8 min. until hard boiled. Peel and finely chop onions. Slice pickles lengthwise and chop.
  3. Add olive oil to a pot over medium heat. Add onions and sugar and stir well. Deglaze with white wine vinegar and add vegetable stock. Add pickles and mustard, season well with salt and pepper, and stir to combine.
  4. When eggs are done cooking, transfer to an ice bath. Peel boiled eggs and chop into medium-sized pieces.
  5. Use a knife to check if the potatoes are done, then peel, and cut into approx. 1-cm/0.4-inch. thick slices. Add potato slices to a large bowl along with eggs, pickles, and vegetable stock mixture and gently stir to combine. Transfer to refrigerator for approx. 30 min. Season again with salt and pepper to taste. Finely chop a handful of fresh chives and add to potato salad. Stir to incorporate. Enjoy!
