Classic Schnitzel

Description

A thin meat cutlet, pounded until tender and then breaded and fried, that is a culinary staple in German-speaking countries and communities.

This recipe is designed for 4 people.

Ingredients

  • 500g veal
  • 4 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 200g flour
  • 200g breadcrums
  • 1 lemon
  • butter for frying
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs and milk.
  2. Cut veal into approx. two finger thick butterflied portions.
  3. Beat out the veal as thinly as possible without making holes in it.
  4. Season cutlets with salt and pepper. Dredge each piece in flour and afterwards in the egg mixture. Set aside.
  5. Next, dredge the cutlets in breadcrumbs.
  6. In a frying pan, fry schnitzel in some butter over medium heat until golden brown and crispy. Season once more with salt. Serve with a lemon wedge and enjoy with some homemade potato salad!
